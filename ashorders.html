"If the Hero of Time was Black" is Ashley Harris' first published chapbook through Weasel Press. It explores the racial and gender nuances that exist between our current world and the world of Hyrule. These poems paint black ancestors, activists and those who survive in white spaces as heroes, detailing their methods of thriving where they do not belong. This book dares to color the hero of origin in the Zelda timeline as black.
